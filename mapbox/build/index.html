Million Neighborhoods

Streets are key to urban life: they carry clean water
and sanitation, electricity and internet, and provide
routes for emergency responders, trash collectors,
and buses. About a sixth of the global population live
in neighborhoods that lack these services, partially
because of limited physical access to street networks.
Million Neighborhoods is an initiative seeking to map
critically underserviced communities and explore
solutions to improve access to streets.
