Streets are key to urban life: they
hold pipes that carry clean water and
sanitation, connect homes to electricity
and internet, and provide routes for
emergency responders, trash collectors,
and buses. About a sixth of the global
population lives in neighborhoods that
lack these services partially due to
insufficient access to street networks.
